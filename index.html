Banana Bread

Easy to make and clean-up is easy too! If you want, you can mix everything in one mixing bowl.
The best bananas to use for banana bread are those that are over-ripe.
It takes approximately 55 minutes to make, including 10 minutes of prep time.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ cup sugar (½ cup if you would like it less sweet, 1 cup if more sweet)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1½ cup of all-purpose flour

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C), and butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the baking soda. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350°F (175°C)
  5. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving. Slice and serve.
